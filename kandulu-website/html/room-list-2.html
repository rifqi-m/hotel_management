About Villa Cocoa

Villa Cocoa offers a luxurious and tranquil retreat for guests seeking a unique experience in Puerto Viejo. Nestled in lush surroundings, this villa combines modern amenities with natural beauty.

Enjoy the spacious living areas, beautifully designed bedrooms, and private outdoor spaces perfect for relaxation and entertainment. Villa Cocoa is equipped with everything you need for a memorable stay.
