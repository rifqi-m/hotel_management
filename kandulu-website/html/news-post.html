Exciting Updates at Villas Kandulu

Welcome to Villas Kandulu

We are thrilled to announce some exciting updates and improvements at Villas Kandulu. Our team has been working tirelessly to enhance your stay and ensure you have the best experience possible. Here’s what’s new:

Our newly renovated Deluxe Rooms now feature modern amenities, stylish decor, and improved facilities to make your stay even more comfortable. We've also added a new Infinity Pool with a breathtaking view of the lush landscapes of Puerto Viejo. Whether you're here for relaxation or adventure, Villas Kandulu has something for everyone.

Upcoming Events and Activities

We're excited to introduce a variety of events and activities for our guests. Join us for Beach Yoga Sessions every morning to start your day with peace and tranquility. Explore the vibrant culture of Puerto Viejo with our guided Local Art and Culture Tours. Don't miss out on our Monthly Culinary Nights featuring local and international chefs showcasing their signature dishes.

Stay tuned for more updates and make sure to check our events calendar for the latest happenings. At Villas Kandulu, we are committed to making your stay unforgettable.

Explore the Beauty of Puerto Viejo

Puerto Viejo is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. At Villas Kandulu, we offer guided tours that take you through the stunning landscapes, rich wildlife, and vibrant culture of the region. Join us for a memorable journey that includes hiking, bird watching, and cultural immersion activities.

Our knowledgeable guides will ensure you have an enriching experience, providing insights and stories about the local environment and communities.

Discover the Hidden Gems

From secluded beaches to hidden waterfalls, our tours cover all the must-see spots and secret locations that only locals know about. Experience the true essence of Puerto Viejo and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Stay tuned for more updates and make sure to check our tours page for the latest offerings. At Villas Kandulu, we are committed to making your stay unforgettable.

Deluxe Rooms and Suites

We are excited to showcase our newly renovated Deluxe Rooms and Suites at Villas Kandulu. Each room is designed with your comfort in mind, featuring luxurious bedding, spacious layouts, and stunning views of the surrounding nature.

Our suites offer additional amenities including private balconies, Jacuzzis, and personalized services to make your stay truly special.

Experience Luxury and Comfort

Each room is equipped with modern conveniences such as high-speed internet, flat-screen TVs, and mini-bars. Our housekeeping team ensures that your room is immaculate and ready for your arrival.

Stay tuned for more updates and make sure to check our rooms page for the latest offerings. At Villas Kandulu, we are committed to making your stay unforgettable.
